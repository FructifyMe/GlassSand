1.0 Executive Summary

This document provides technical analysis for PGS-100 (Pulverized Glass Sand), an amorphous silicate aggregate derived from post-consumer soda-lime glass. Processed via impact crushing and screening, PGS-100 is engineered to replace natural sand in civil engineering, filtration, and agricultural applications. The material is chemically inert, non-hazardous, and free of sharp edges due to the tumbling action during processing.

5.0 Regulatory & Environmental Compliance

TCLP (Leaching) Status

Tested via EPA Method 1311. Results indicate non-hazardous classification. Lead and heavy metal leaching levels are below regulatory thresholds for groundwater safety.

Worker Safety (Silica)

Contains < 1% Crystalline Silica (Respirable). Material is predominantly amorphous. Standard dust control measures recommended (OSHA 1926.1153).

5.1 Approved Applications

  • Pipe Bedding: Meets compaction requirements for non-structural utility trenches.
  • Filtration Media: Effective size (D10) and Uniformity Coefficient (Cu) optimized for tertiary wastewater treatment.
  • Abrasives: "Medium" grade suitable for blast media (SSPC-AB 1).
